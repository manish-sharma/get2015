Volkswagen Polo

Volkswagen is one such company that has always stood up to the expectations of the automobile aficionados. In India, it is selling premium products, but struggling to reach its sales target. It has several models in its fleet among which, Volkswagen Polo hatchback is its best selling product. Perhaps, it is the only model in the hatchback segment of India to receive 4-star NCAP ratings, which explains about its standards. In a bid to tackle the competition, the manufacturer has updated its top end Highline variants by adding a few additional features. The remaining trims like Trendline and Comfortline are kept as they are.
