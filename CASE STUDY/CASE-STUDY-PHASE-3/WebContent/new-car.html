BMW X3 xDrive20d Expedition

The all new BMW X3 is available in two variants with a diesel engine as standard. Its entry level trim is badged as BMW X3 xDrive20d Expedition and is equipped with a refined 2.0-litre, twin-power turbo diesel mill. It has the ability to produce 190bhp and a commanding torque output of 400Nm, which is 6bhp and 20Nm more than its earlier version.
