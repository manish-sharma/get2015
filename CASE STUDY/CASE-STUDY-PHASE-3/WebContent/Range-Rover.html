Land Rover Range Rover Sport

In the Indian car market the Jaguar Land Rover is one of the most reputed car brand, which is famous for their rugged sports utility vehicles. Among the various models in its portfolio, Land Rover Range Rover Sport is an impressive sports utility vehicle that is offered with both petrol and diesel engine options for the buyers to choose from. The company is selling this model in quite a few variants, which are S, SE, HSE and Autobiography. Its diesel variants are powered by a 3.0-litre V6 diesel engine that produces a maximum power of 288.3bhp in combination with a peak torque of 600Nm.
